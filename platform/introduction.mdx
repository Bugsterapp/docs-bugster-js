Bugster helps you automate your testing process by capturing real user flows and converting them into maintainable tests. This guide will help you understand and navigate the main features of the platform.

## Getting Started

1. **Test Flows**: Start by exploring your [existing flows](/platform/test-flows/existing-flows) captured from real user interactions
2. **Flow Groups**: [Organize your flows](/platform/flow-groups) into logical groups for better management
3. **Settings**: Configure your [project settings](/platform/project-settings) to customize Bugster for your needs
