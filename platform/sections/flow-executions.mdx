## Viewing Individual Executions

When you click on an individual execution, you'll get access to a detailed view of everything that happened during the test run:

### Steps and Screenshots
Each step performed by the agent is documented with:
- Detailed action description
- Screenshot capture of the action
- Step status (success/failure)
- Execution time

### Additional Information Tabs
The execution view provides multiple tabs to analyze the test run:
- **Network**: View all network requests and responses
- **Console**: Check browser console logs and messages
- **Screenshots**: Browse all captured screenshots in a gallery view

## Sharing Executions

You can easily share your test executions with team members:

1. Open the execution you want to share
2. Click on the "Share" button in the top right
3. Toggle the execution to "Public"
4. Copy and share the generated link

Your colleagues can access the execution details without needing to log in.
